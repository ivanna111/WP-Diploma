Why is our Club the best solution for learning English?

Learning through play makes the anxiety go away! This is NOT a regular English class! It’s a fun and safe space where kids have the opportunity to learn and play through communication. By using language to connect with their peers in an environment they are eager to be part of, children are less afraid of making mistakes.
Learning has now got a practical purpose! Youth facilitators encourage communication by creating a reinforcing atmosphere that helps kids converse with each other. When language becomes a medium for a friendly discussion, kids start to look forward to speaking.
The Speaking Club paves the way toward Future STEM and leadership projects. Once they perfect their conversational English skills, Club members can come aboard on the Explorer projects, which tackle their soft and hard skills while partaking in an international atmosphere.
